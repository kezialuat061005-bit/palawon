PUERTO PRINCESA UNDERGROUND RIVER

The Puerto Princesa Underground River (also known as Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park) is a major natural attraction in Palawan. It features a river flowing through a cave system beneath a mountain, with dramatic limestone formations, wildlife (bats, swiftlets), and sections open to public boat tours. The total cave system is around 8.2 km long, though only part of it is accessible to visitors by boat.

TRAVEL TIP

Book your permit well in advance, especially in high season. The “no permit, no entry” policy is enforced. If possible, arrive early at Sabang (or from Puerto Princesa) to avoid long waits. If you're prone to motion sickness, be mindful: the van rides have twists, and small boats can get a bit bumpy. Check weather ahead because rough seas or heavy rain can cause delays or cancellations.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Expect a van ride through scenic landscapes, possibly winding roads. Once at Sabang, there may be waiting times for permits or boat access. The boat ride into the cave is often quiet, dark, with limited lighting — you’ll see rock formations, hear dripping water, bats, maybe birds. The cave affects humidity and temperature, so expect a cool damp environment. Outside, Sabang is more tropical, warm, and lush.

GUIDE

Tours typically include van transfer from Puerto Princesa City to Sabang to boat to cave entrance to paddle/boat inside cave with a local guide.
Safety gear is provided (life vest, helmet, sometimes flashlight). Guides will point out cave formations, bats, etc.
There is regulated capacity inside the cave; only certain stretches are accessible to public boats.

BEST TIME TO GO

The dry season — November through May — is the best period for both destinations. Calm seas, clearer skies, fewer cancellations. Peak months are December-April (higher crowds). Shoulder months like November and May are good for a balance. Avoid heavy rainy months (June to October) when tours could be disrupted.

WHAT TO BRING

Lightweight Swimwear

Waterproof Bag

Insect Repellent

Good Footwear

flashlight/Headlamp

Cash

Sunscreen, Hat, and Sunglasses
