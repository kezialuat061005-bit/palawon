PalaWON:
Your Winning Escape

Welcome to PalaWON: Your Winning Escape — a travel blog dedicated to uncovering the beauty, culture, and adventures that make Palawan one of the most breathtaking destinations in the Philippines.

PalaWON was born from our shared love for exploration and the belief that every journey is a win. Whether you’re wandering through hidden lagoons, tasting local cuisine, or soaking up the island sunshine, we believe travel should always feel rewarding — not just in memories, but in the moments that change you.

Here at PalaWON, we don’t just share destinations — we share experiences. Our goal is to guide fellow travelers with honest tips, local insights, and inspiring stories that help you plan your perfect Palawan adventure.

Vision

To be a trusted and inspiring travel companion that celebrates the natural beauty and cultural richness of Palawan, while promoting sustainable and responsible tourism.

Mission

To inspire every traveler to discover the wonders of Palawan with a spirit of curiosity, respect, and joy, making every trip a true winning escape.

Our Story

What started as a simple passion for travel turned into a mission to share Palawan’s best–kept treasures with the world. From crystal–clear lagoons to vibrant towns, we aim to give travelers a glimpse of what makes this island paradise so special.

Meet the Team

We are a group of travel enthusiasts and storytellers passionate about adventure and exploration. As travelers ourselves, we understand the thrill of discovering new places — and we’re here to make that journey easier, more meaningful, and unforgettable for you.

Because here at PalaWON: Your Winning Escape, every story begins with one simple goal — to wander, to win, and to experience life at its most beautiful.
